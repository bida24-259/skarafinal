Discover why soy wax outperforms paraffin in every way...

Soy wax burns at a cooler temperature than paraffin, which means:

  • 30% longer burn time
  • No toxic toluene or benzene emissions
  • Cleaner walls and furniture (no black soot)
  • Biodegradable and water-soluble for easy cleanup

Our soy is grown by American farmers using sustainable practices, supporting local agriculture.
